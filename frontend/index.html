About Prime Minister's Internship Scheme

Background & Objectives

The Prime Minister's Internship Scheme was launched to address the employability gap among Indian youth. It aims to provide practical work experience, enhance skills, and improve the career prospects of young graduates across the country, with special focus on rural, tribal, and underserved communities.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21-24 years
  • Education: Bachelor's degree or Diploma in relevant field
  • Family income: Up to ‚Çπ8 lakh per annum
  • Indian citizen with valid Aadhaar card
  • Not currently employed or enrolled in full-time education

Benefits

  • Monthly stipend of ‚Çπ5,000
  • One-time assistance of ‚Çπ6,000
  • 12 months of practical work experience
  • Industry exposure with leading companies
  • Certificate of completion
  • Skill development and training
